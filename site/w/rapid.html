rapid

形容詞/ˈræpɪd/CEFR B1頻度#2386
  1. 【形容詞】速く起こる、素早く進む、敏速な（happening or moving very quickly）
  2. 【形容詞】短時間での急速な変化や増加（rapidly changing or increasing in a short time）
  3. 【名詞】川の流れが速い区間、急流（a part of a river where the water rushes quickly）

⛏Building a rapid mine shaft saves time and resources.
