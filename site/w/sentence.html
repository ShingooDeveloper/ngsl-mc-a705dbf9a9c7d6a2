sentence

名詞・動詞/ˈsentəns/CEFR A1頻度#1096
  1. 【名詞】（文法的な単位の）文（…a string of words forming a statement）
  2. 【名詞】判決、有罪判決（…an official decision by a court about punishment）
  3. 【名詞】刑期、懲役（…a period of time to spend in prison）
  4. 【動詞】判決を下す（…to tell someone officially what their punishment is）
